About

This small demo site is built with Twitter Bootstrap, AngularJS, Django and Django REST Framework.

The data models are as follows:

Polyhedron
A 3 dimensional shape onto which images can be mapped

Texture
An image from which a clipping can be applied to the faces of a Polyhedron. A Texture can be applied to many shapes

TextureLine
A grouping of textures that expresses a particular "style" i.e. spacey, earthy. A shape may or may not have Textures in a particular TextureLine

TextureImplementation
An application of a Texture onto a Polyhedron

PolyhedronProduct
A particular manifestiation of the TextureImplementation in the form of a product i.e. made of cardstock or backlight media
